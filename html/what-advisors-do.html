Academic Advisors Do

Act as your advisor for your stay at CSU. The only way to change your advisor is to change your major

Distribute advising codes needed to register for classes

Send out newsletters related to your major. This includes things like talks, seminars, carrer events, changes to cirriculum and more

Map out which courses to take and when

Advise you if you are struggling with classes or professors

Help you find resources on campus regarding: studying/tutoring/academic skills, physical, mental and emotional health, food, financial aid, and more

Help students connect with student organizations for professional or recreational purposes and help students seeking ways to connect with other students of their identities.

*In some cases they can offered overrides for courses though this varies department-by-department

Academic Advisors Do Not

Register for classes for you

Pick out classes for you, rather they will give you a list of classes to pick from to satisfy graduation requirments

Approve change of major into another department, this is done by admissions. More information can be found here

Evaluate transfer credits or study abroad forms, this is done by the registrar. More information can be found here
